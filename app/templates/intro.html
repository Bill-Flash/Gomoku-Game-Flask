Introduction

  1. 1. Rules
  2. The two sides use black and white pieces respectively. Empty board opening. Black first, white, alternate down time, each time only a piece of the chess. The first black piece can be placed at any intersection on the board. Pieces are placed at the intersection of the straight and horizontal lines of the board. The one who forms the line of five pieces first wins.

  4. 2.1 Local Mode: Two players play on the same computer face to face. No registration required. Results do not count towards the ranklist.
  5. 2.2 Online Mode: Players randomly match with other online players in real time. Registration is required. Results count towards the ranklist.
